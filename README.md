🌍 Future of Work: A 7-Year Global Salary & AI Analysis (2020-2026)
💡 Why this project?
As a Computer Science student, I've always been fascinated by how data can predict our future careers. I didn't just want to see "who earns what" today; I wanted to see where the world is heading by 2026, especially with the massive wave of AI automation.

This dashboard is my attempt to turn raw, messy workforce data into a clear story that helps professionals and students like me make better career decisions.

🧠 The Challenge & The Logic
This wasn't just "drag and drop." To make this work, I had to:

Clean the Chaos: I spent hours in Power Query fixing inconsistent data types and handling missing values (especially around those tricky AI risk percentages).

Connect the Dots: Created custom DAX measures to calculate a "Prosperity Index"—a way to measure not just how much we earn, but how high the quality of life is in different sectors.

Predict the Future: Built a trend analysis model that projects salary growth into 2026, giving a glimpse of the post-AI job market.

🚀 What can you find here?
The Global Map: A bird’s eye view of where the best opportunities are.

The Salary Evolution: A line chart that tells the story of how our income is changing year by year.

The AI Reality Check: A deep dive into automation risks—showing that while Healthcare is safe and high-paying ($99K+), other sectors need to adapt fast!

The Education Gap: A visual breakdown of how much that degree actually impacts your paycheck.

🛠️ Tools of the Trade
Power BI: For the magic visuals and interactive storytelling.

Excel: My reliable companion for the initial data sourcing.

Analytical Thinking: The most important tool I used to decide which metrics actually matter to a business.
